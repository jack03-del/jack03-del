Why I Love Surfing

Surfing is more than just a sport to me, it's been a part of who I am since I was born. There's nothing quite like the feeling of catching the perfect wave, the connection with the ocean, and the peace that comes from being out on the water. Whether it's dawn patrol or sunset sessions, every moment in the water is pure happiness for me.

Essential Surfing Gear

Here's my recommended gear list for different skill levels:

  1. Beginner Setup
    • Soft-top longboard (8-9 feet)
    • Leash (ankle or calf)
    • Wetsuit (appropriate for water temp)
    • Surf wax or traction pad
  2. Intermediate Setup
    • Funboard or fish (6-7 feet)
    • Multiple fins (thruster or quad setup)
    • Booties for reef breaks
    • Surf watch for tide tracking
  3. Advanced Setup
    • Shortboard (5'8" - 6'2")
    • Travel board bag
    • GoPro for capturing surf sessions
    • Surf forecasting app subscription
